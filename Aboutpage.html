WORK ABOUT

Cong Minh Do is a graphic designer completing a Master of Design (Graphic) at the Victorian College of the Arts (VCA). He studied a Bachelor of Graphic Design at the School of Visual Arts, New York and completed a Bachelor of Design (Graphic) at the Victorian College of the Arts (VCA). He is passionate about design with illustrations that hold a layered, contrasting message within. He has worked on many design jobs in his home country Vietnam as well as with associations based in Melbourne. His credits at the VCA include working with Pride Cup.
